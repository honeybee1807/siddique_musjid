Who We Are

A Foundation Built on Faith

The Siddique Charitable Foundation NPC is established to address the urgent need for Islamic facilities and educational opportunities in disadvantaged communities. We strive to create spaces that inspire spiritual growth, provide access to quality education, and unite communities through shared values.

VISION

To establish a unified, resilient, and empowered Muslim community by building sustainable institutions that enrich lives through faith, education, and service.

MISSION

Our mission is to develop inclusive and transformative spaces that nurture spiritual well-being, advance educational opportunities, and foster unity. By upholding the values of faith, compassion, and service, we aim to empower individuals and strengthen communities.

KEY PROJECT  01

Building a Musjid

Amajuba Park · Newcastle · KwaZulu-Natal

Whoever builds a mosque for Allah, Allah will reward them with a place in Paradise.

— Al-Bukhari

The Siddique Charitable Foundation is constructing a new Musjid on 2,135 square metres of land in Amajuba Park — a neighbourhood currently without any Islamic facilities, adjacent to three other suburbs. This Musjid will serve as a spiritual hub for the community, providing a dedicated space for Salaah, Islamic education, and communal gatherings. It aims to foster unity and support among the local Muslim population.

KEY PROJECT  02

Kwamathukuza Madressa

1177 Umziki Street · Kwamathukuza · Newcastle

The Kwamathukuza Madressa provides a dedicated space for Islamic education in a disadvantaged community with a growing demand for Islamic knowledge. Currently serving 35 enrolled students, the Madressa nurtures young minds with the values and teachings of Islam, serving as a cornerstone for spiritual growth and encouraging Shahada in the surrounding population.
